Oooh-wee!  These past two weeks were a struggle,
<abbr title="not gonna lie">ngl</abbr>!  Building this blog has been a
back-burner idea of mine for years, and I’m so proud to have finally got
something set up.  49 people read my
<a href="https://christianhain.com/blog/week-in-review-2024-w21/" title="Week in Review: 2024-W21">
  original post</a>!
WAY-YYY more than the zero I was expecting.  But then, in the blink of an eye,
my intrusive thoughts jeopardized everything.

I’m not going to spill any tea; this isn’t that kind of blog, but I want to
take a moment to remind us to pay attention to our mental health.  I slipped
into a week-long funk because I was too afraid to speak about the weight on my
heart.  My mind went to the worst-case scenario, and I tried to play it “cool”
even though I could hardly focus on anything else; my motivation to write
evaporated.

Even this feels forced.

But I have to remind myself that I want this— I want to lean into my authentic
self.  There’s still some healing to do, but I’ll get back to writing in a few
weeks' time.  Let me take care of some things over here first.
